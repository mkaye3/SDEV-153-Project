Petition Overview

We, the undersigned, call for stronger enforcement of traffic laws and meaningful consequences for dangerous driving behaviors that continue to put lives at risk every day. Traffic accidents remain a leading cause of injury and death, many of which are entirely preventable. Reckless driving, impaired driving, distracted driving, and excessive speeding have created unsafe conditions on our roads that demand immediate action.

Our communities deserve safer streets, and it is the responsibility of lawmakers and enforcement agencies to ensure that traffic laws are taken seriously and enforced effectively. By implementing stricter penalties and improving enforcement, we can deter dangerous behavior and significantly reduce the number of preventable accidents.

We urge policymakers and local authorities to take the following actions:

  • 🚔 1. Stronger DUI Penalties
    • Increase fines for DUI offenses
    • Mandatory license suspension for first-time offenders
    • Longer incarceration for repeat offenders
    • Required rehabilitation programs for offenders
  • ⚡ 2. Stricter Speeding Enforcement
    • Higher fines for excessive speeding (e.g., 20+ mph over limit)
    • Increased use of speed enforcement technologies
    • Immediate penalties for reckless driving speeds
  • 📱 3. Crackdown on Distracted Driving
    • Increased fines for texting while driving
    • Primary enforcement laws (officers can pull drivers over solely for phone use)
    • Public awareness campaigns about distracted driving dangers
  • 🚗 4. Repeat Offender Accountability
    • Harsher penalties for repeat traffic violations
    • Points-based systems leading to license suspension
    • Mandatory driving education courses for repeat offenders
  • 📊 5. Increased Traffic Enforcement Presence
    • More patrol officers focused on traffic safety
    • Strategic enforcement in high-accident areas
    • Regular safety checkpoints where appropriate
