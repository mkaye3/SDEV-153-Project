What We Stand For

Our mission is to improve road safety and reduce traffic-related accidents through stronger enforcement of traffic laws, including increased fines and appropriate incarceration for serious and repeat offenders. We believe that accountability on the road is essential to protecting lives and creating safer communities. By discouraging reckless and dangerous driving behaviors, we aim to promote responsibility, awareness, and respect among all drivers. Ultimately, our goal is to reduce preventable crashes and ensure that every person on the road can travel safely and confidently.

The Importance of This Petition

Traffic safety data highlights the seriousness of the issue we are addressing. For example, approximately 29% of all traffic accidents involve a DUI, showing the significant role impaired driving plays in roadway incidents. In addition to this, thousands of crashes each year are caused by speeding, distracted driving, and other preventable behaviors. These statistics emphasize the urgent need for stronger enforcement and greater accountability on our roads.
